About Us

Welcome to Huron Robotics! We're a competitive robotics team that strives for excellence, supports one another, and gives back to our community. Huron Robotics was started to bring additional STEM opportunities to Huron High School to build a brighter future for our students. Through our mentorship and student leadership, we strive to be a world class robotics team in the future.
